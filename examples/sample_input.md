# Sample Input

This is a fictional, shortened input example. It is not from a CET-6 paper.

## Passage

Many students try to memorize long word lists before an exam. However, the students who review words in context often remember them longer. A useful review note does not only record the meaning of a word. It also records why the word mattered in a question.

## Question

What does the passage suggest about effective vocabulary review?

## Options

A. Students should memorize every word before reading.

B. Words are easier to remember when reviewed with context.

C. Exam questions never require vocabulary knowledge.

D. Long word lists are always more useful than short notes.

## Correct Answer

B
